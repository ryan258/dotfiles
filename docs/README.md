# Documentation Index

Welcome to your dotfiles guides. This is a system of tools that helps you get things done. It has 66 scripts, 21 helper files, 13 AI tools, a blog builder, an AI coach, and a special easy mode for days when your brain feels foggy.

---

## Start Here (5 Minutes)

```bash
dotfiles-check              # Check that your computer is ready
startday                    # Your morning check-in and AI coaching
todo top && status          # Pick your most important chore and start working
goodevening                 # Save your work and reflect on your day
```

Having a brain-fog day? Just type `ap` and press Enter.
